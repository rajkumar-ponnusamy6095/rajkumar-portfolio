Indium Software Private Limited

Indium Software Private Limited is a global technology services company, established in 1999 and headquartered in Chennai. Prior to the adoption of AI and GenAI capabilities, the company focused on core services such as software testing, quality assurance, application development, and data analytics. It served clients across industries like banking, healthcare, retail, and gaming. Known for its strong delivery frameworks, domain expertise, and emphasis on quality, Indium supported digital transformation through agile methodologies and reliable software solutions. It provided opportunities for professionals to work on diverse projects, enabling robust growth and a collaborative work culture across global delivery centers.

Aigilx Health Technologies Private Limited

Aigilx Health Technologies Private Limited is a Chennai-based health IT company focused on advancing healthcare interoperability and seamless data exchange. It offers the RapidFire platform, which includes a robust FHIR server and HIE modules that support integration across electronic health records (EHR), labs, payers, and behavioral health systems. Aigilx specializes in healthcare data standards such as HL7 v2, CCDA, X12, and FHIR, enabling secure and scalable clinical data management. Through strategic partnerships and cutting-edge solutions, the company empowers healthcare organizations to streamline information flow, enhance patient care coordination, and achieve compliance with modern interoperability requirements.
